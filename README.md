Introducing AutoTracker: Your Vehicle's Ultimate Co-Pilot

Take the hassle out of vehicle management. AutoTracker is a beautifully designed, fully automated app that logs your trips, tracks your expenses, and keeps your vehicle's maintenance history perfectly organized. Built with a sleek, modern Glassmorphic interface, AutoTracker does the heavy lifting in the background so you can focus on the drive.

🌟 KEY FEATURES

📍 Zero-Touch Automated Trip Tracking
Never forget to log a drive again. Simply link your vehicle’s Bluetooth or Wi-Fi network. AutoTracker will automatically start recording your route, distance, and speed the moment you connect, and stop when you park. It runs seamlessly in the background!

🏝️ Native Dynamic Island & Fluid Cloud Support
Keep your eyes on the road. AutoTracker features deep OS integration, seamlessly displaying your live trip distance, current speed, and top speed directly inside the Dynamic Island / Fluid Cloud status bar pill on supported devices (like OxygenOS 14+).

🚘 Android Auto Integration
Take AutoTracker on the road! View your active vehicles, garage status, and trip logs directly from your car's physical dashboard head unit. Seamlessly hand over tracking from your phone to your car.

🛠️ Complete Service & Expense Logs
Throw away the paper receipts. Log every refueling, oil change, service appointment, and custom modification. AutoTracker calculates your total investment and provides a clean breakdown of where your money is going.

⏰ Smart Maintenance Reminders
Never miss a crucial renewal or service. Set custom reminders based on your Odometer readings or specific dates for Engine Oil changes, PUCC (Pollution) certificates, and Insurance renewals.

☁️ Secure Cloud Sync & Backup
Log in with your Google account to securely sync your entire garage and trip history to the cloud. Upgrade your phone? Your data instantly restores. Prefer offline? You can easily export your entire database locally.

🎨 Futuristic Design
Enjoy a premium, visually stunning "Glassmorphism" interface with dynamic styling and full Dark Mode support that looks great on any screen.

🔒 Privacy First
Your data belongs to you. AutoTracker requires background location specifically for automated trip tracking, and we clearly explain how and why it is used. You have full control to export or permanently delete your cloud data at any time.
